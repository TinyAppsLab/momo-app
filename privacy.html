Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Your privacy matters

Momo is designed as a private place for your worries and thoughts. The app does not require an account.

Information stored in the app

All worries and thoughts are stored locally on the user's iPhone. Momo does not transmit, collect, or have access to the content entered into the app.

If the app is deleted, locally stored content may also be deleted and may not be recoverable.

Data collection

Momo does not collect the content of your worries or thoughts. Momo does not sell personal information and does not use written content for advertising.

Payments

Momo is available as a one-time purchase on the Apple App Store. All payments are securely processed by Apple.

Third-party services

Momo currently does not use third-party services to process the content you enter into the app. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app or its features change. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact abc@abc.pl.

Review this page if the app later adds analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, accounts, advertising, or subscriptions.
