Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Momo can be used without creating an account.

Can I add more thoughts to an existing worry?

Yes. Open a worry and add another thought whenever something else comes to mind.

Can I delete a worry?

Yes. You can remove a worry when you are ready to let it go.
