Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Momo can be used without creating an account.

Where are my worries stored?

They are stored locally on your iPhone. Momo does not transmit or have access to the content you write.

Can I add more thoughts to an existing worry?

Yes. Open a worry and add another thought whenever something else comes to mind.

Can I remove a worry?

Yes. You can delete a worry whenever you are ready to let it go.

Is Momo a subscription?

No. Momo is offered as a one-time App Store purchase.
